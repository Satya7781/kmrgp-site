Lineage Integrity

How Our Ancestry Gotra Verification Works

To preserve our lineage guidelines and traditional values, every applicant must pass a strict verification process overseen by respected Mewar community elders.

1

Gotra Details Submission

Applicants provide complete gotra charts covering Self, Mother, Paternal Grandmother (Dadi), and Maternal Grandmother (Nani) for full lineage mapping.

2

Elders Manual Review

Mewada Rajput community elders manually cross-verify all details with ancestral charts (Vanshavali) to confirm authentic and undisputed lineages.

3

Verified Profile Badge

Upon elder approval, the profile receives an official Verified Shield badge and becomes visible to other registered and verified community members.
